# Live Music & Performing Arts

## Creative Crossroads

Experience the creative pulse of a community that music legends Tom Petty, Stephen Stills, and Bo Diddley have called home. From world-class performances to intimate local stages, Gainesville and Alachua County set the scene for unforgettable live entertainment.

Spend an evening at one of the area's renowned theaters --- including the historic Hippodrome Theatre, Curtis M. Phillips Center for the Performing Arts and Constans Theatre --- where Broadway shows, ballets and orchestral masterpieces take center stage. For a charming community theater experience, don't miss a production at the High Springs Playhouse, a local favorite.

Enjoy Dance Alive National Ballet and the Gainesville Orchestra performances, or gather for free outdoor concerts at Bo Diddley Plaza. Whether you're drawn to classical, contemporary, or homegrown talent, Alachua County's dynamic arts scene offers something for every audience.

## Music Venues

### Bo Diddley Plaza

Enjoy the Free Fridays Concert Series (April-September), music festivals and other community events located in the heart of Downtown Gainesville.

### Heartwood Soundstage

Catch your next favorite band at this jewel box of a venue featuring an intimate indoor listening room and outdoor stage. Stay up-to-date with their robust (and well-curated) schedule. Be sure to check out Heartwood's "Community Night Market and Music" every Wednesday night.

## Signature Festivals

### FEST: Punks Welcome

Rock out at FEST, Gainesville's signature punk rock music festival, drawing thousands of visitors from around the world each October for three days of performances across multiple downtown venues.

### Tom Petty Weekend

Celebrate one of rock's greatest icons in the city where his music began. The fall Tom Petty Weekend honors Gainesville's legendary hometown rocker with performances by national, regional and local acts.


### Dance Alive National Ballet

Experience world-class ballet in the heart of Florida as Dance Alive National Ballet brings award-winning dancers and distinctive choreography to North Central Florida.

### The Gainesville Orchestra

Discover the rich 40-year legacy of The Gainesville Orchestra, where traditional and contemporary music comes alive through innovative collaborations, family-friendly performances and educational initiatives.

## Theaters

### Acrosstown Repertory Theatre

This intimate, 50-seat theatre has shows that range from drama to comedy, period piece to musical. The Acrosstown has hosted regional premieres, touring productions, youth performances, and produces a new works play festival every spring.

### Gainesville Community Playhouse Theatre

The Gainesville Community Playhouse is one of the oldest community theaters in Florida producting quality plays and musicals since 1927. GCP is housed in the Vam York Theater, a 210-seat, state-of-the-art facility.

### High Springs Playhouse

The High Springs Playhouse is a small, intimate community theater located in historic downtown High Springs, Florida, where local performers have been bringing live entertainment to audiences since 1993.

### Hippodrome Theatre

The Hippodrome Theatre, Gainesville's professional theater company, produces contemporary plays and classics throughout the year. Its cinema space screens independent and foreign films. The iconic building was constructed as a federal building and post office in 1911 and transformed into a performing arts space in 1973.

### Curtis C. Phillips Center for the Performing Arts

Step inside the Phillips Center at UF, where Broadway hits and world-class performers light up a gorgeous 1,700-seat theater. From symphony orchestras to dance troupes, every show feels intimate thanks to perfect sightlines and crystal-clear acoustics that bring you right into the action.

### University of Florida Performing Arts Venues

UF's performing arts venues range from the adaptable Squitieri Studio Theatre to the historic University Auditorium with its grand pipe organ and perfect acoustics. The Phillips Center stage transforms for intimate UpStage jazz performances, while the Baughman Center offers a serene lakeside space with soaring windows.

### Star Center Theater

Star Center Theatre is a performing arts center that empowers the community through comprehensive arts education and performance opportunities. Through its diverse programs, including youth initiatives, adult ensembles, and summer camps, the theater creates inclusive spaces for performers of all ages.
