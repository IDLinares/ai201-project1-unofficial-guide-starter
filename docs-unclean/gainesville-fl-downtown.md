## You'll Love These 11 Things to Do in Downtown Gainesville, FL

### MORNING

#### **Cade Museum**

The [Cade Museum](https://sweetwaterinn.com/blog/cade-museum/) is a great way to start your day in Gainesville. You can participate in exciting programs and events, admire creative installments, and educate yourself on new technologies.

#### **Depot Park**

Once you're done exploring the museum, go for a lovely stroll at [Depot Park](https://sweetwaterinn.com/blog/depot-park-in-gainesville/). This new addition to the city is ideal for a break in nature and is right around the corner from the Cade. They even have a conservation area with wetlands and wildlife!

#### **Matheson History Museum**

Travel back in time in one of the [best museums in Gainesville](https://sweetwaterinn.com/blog/gainesville-fl-museums/). The [Matheson History Museum](https://sweetwaterinn.com/blog/matheson-history-museum/) dives into Florida's rich history and showcases the rich cultural significance of the state. Their exhibits rotate regularly, so you might want to go back even if you've visited it before.

### LUNCH

#### **Big Lou's N.Y. -\*\***Style Pizza\*\*

By lunchtime, you will be starving. If you want to try one of the best pizzas in Gainesville, head over to [Big Lou's](https://www.facebook.com/BigLousGainesville/)! Their crunchy crust and tomato sauce are to die for. The garlic rolls are also a must-try. Trust us—you won't regret it! 

### AFTERNOON

#### **Union Street Station**

Shopping is a great way to digest, so once you're done eating your delicious Big Lou's pizza, go to [Union Street Station](http://unionstreetstation.com/) and indulge in a little retail therapy. It's one of the coolest spots in town. Music lovers can thumb through old vinyl records at Hear Again, while art collectors and enthusiasts can browse the selection at the Artisans Guild Gallery.

#### **Third House Books & Coffee**

Are you a bookworm? If so, you need to stop by [Third House Books & Coffee](https://www.thirdhousebooks.com/). This charming independent bookstore is a great place to encounter works by undiscovered modern writers. Do you want to know the best part? Once you find your book, you can order a coffee and start reading right there! 

### DINNER

#### **Boca Fiesta**

One of the coolest dinner spots downtown is by far [Boca Fiesta](http://www.bocafiesta.com/). This Tex-Mex restaurant is a big part of Gainesville's history. Back in the 1990s, it used to be the famous Hardback Cafe, where numerous rock bands played every week. Did you know that the actor River Phoenix (brother of Joaquin Phoenix) used to play there all the time with his band Aleka's Attic? Pay close attention to the eclectic decor and you might spot some plastic dinosaurs hanging on the ceiling beams.

#### **Manuel's Vintage Room**

For the ultimate [romantic dining experience](https://sweetwaterinn.com/blog/romantic-restaurants-in-gainesville-fl/), book a table at [Manuel's Vintage Room](http://places.singleplatform.com/manuels-vintage-room/menu?ref=google). They serve rich, classic Italian flavors that are absolutely mind--blowing. Make sure you pair your dish with a fine glass of wine from Manuel's private cellar.

### NIGHTTIME

#### **The Arcade Bar**

Who doesn't love an arcade? Play all your childhood games at[ The Arcade Bar](https://sweetwaterinn.com/blog/arcade-bar-gainesville/). With three floors of the bar to cover, you can spend all night showing off your competitive side while indulging in a drink (or two) in between.

#### **Hardback Cafe**

Remember how we told you that Boca Fiesta used to be a live music venue? Well, after being closed for more than 20 years, the original owner officially reopened the [Hardback Cafe](https://www.facebook.com/hardbackgainesville/) a few streets down and regularly hosts local and passing musicians. The venue is small and features an authentic, intimate atmosphere that music lovers will adore.

#### **Rockey's Dueling Piano Bar**

[Rockey's Dueling Piano Bar](https://sweetwaterinn.com/blog/rockeys-dueling-piano-bar/) is one of the most interesting bars in town. It's a fabulous spot to listen to talented pianists flex their skills on stage while performing audience requests. Don't forget to ask about drink specials!

#### **Loosey's Downtown**

If it's a delicious draft beer you crave, then [Loosey's Downtown](http://ldt.looseys.com/) has what you're looking for. There's nothing like pairing a frothy beer with live music and entertainment. Catch a local show or team up for trivia while sharing a cold one with your friends.
